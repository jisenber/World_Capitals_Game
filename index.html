Jacob's World Capitals Game

HOW TO PLAY
  • Click on a continent you choose

  • Enter the country capitals of the countries listed

    • You don't have to go in any order,
      or fill them all out if you don't know!

  • When you have typed in a capital, press ENTER
    or click, "I Know This!"

  • See whether you got it right by looking at the
    counter on the right! you can reset your score anytime

  • Thanks for playing! (Click 'close' to continue)
